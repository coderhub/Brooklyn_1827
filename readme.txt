Title: Brooklyn in the year of 1827

** Works in Firefox and Google Chrome updated browsers **

https://brooklynmap.herokuapp.com/index.html

This 3d model of Brooklyn shows the land occupied or sold in 1827. The blue represents occupied space and the tan color represents undeveloped or unsold land plots.

Click on index.html to view the interactive map.
